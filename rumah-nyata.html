Rumah Nyata

Project type
Landed residential
Location
Jakarta, Indonesia
Scope
Interior design + project management

Rumah Nyata is a 900 sqm, three-storey family home in Jakarta, shaped by quiet conviction. Soft yet assured, the house expresses boldness through subtle architectural gestures — slanted walls, gentle curves, and rounded edges that guide movement and light. Each space carries its own colour palette and character, thoughtfully attuned to its occupants. Together, they create a layered home where contrast feels calm, and luxury reveals itself in restraint and intention.
