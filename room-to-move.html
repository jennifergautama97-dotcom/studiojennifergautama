Room to Move

Project type
Residential - BTO
Location
Singapore
Scope
Interior design

This flat doesn't announce what it's done, even though it moved a whole room to prove a point. The old master gave up its size to become a study you could disappear into, and a wardrobe deep enough to forget what season it is. The common room, in turn, learned to hold a bed like it always meant to. Pale oak leans against darker walnut without asking permission. A marble island holds the kitchen's only real drama. Everything else stays quiet, the way conviction usually does.
