What you ask for isn't always what you need.

Hey! I'm Jennifer - architecture and interior trained, with years across commercial and residential work, from cafes and wine bars to HDB flats and landed homes. A room isn't just a room; it's what you actually live in every day, so I take it seriously. Everyone's taste is different, and my job is turning yours into something real - getting it right matters more to me than getting it done fast. I'm a little obsessed with the gap between what people ask for and what they actually need (a bigger kitchen island is usually really about feeling like the host you picture yourself being). Outside work, I'm climbing, reading, or sketching with my hands - and I treat clients like friends, because the best work happens when we're building it together.
