Held Over

Project type
Residential — BTO
Location
Singapore
Scope
Interior design

Chevron underfoot, walnut across the counters, a green sofa holding its ground by the window — this one borrows from a decade most people have stopped reaching for. Sconces flank the marble like it's still worth lighting properly. Wishbone chairs pull up to a table built for staying a while. None of it tries to look new. It just refuses to look tired.
